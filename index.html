Sleep Apnea

More than 18 million Americans suffer from sleep apnea and many are undiagnosed. With this site, we hope to help you gain a better understanding of this disease, its health ramifications, and some treatment options.

Learn More

What is sleep apnea?

Sleep apnea is when you stop breathing while asleep, due to a physiological obstruction to your airway. A sleep study, in which a physician monitors your vital signs while you sleep, is performed to determine if you have sleep apnea. If you stop breathing for at least 10 seconds at a time for 5 times per hour, then you would be diagnosed with sleep apnea.
